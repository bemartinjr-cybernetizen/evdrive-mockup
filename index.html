FAQ

Questions buyers actually ask.

Do you sell battery modules to individuals, or only OEMs?
Both. We supply performance-focused modules and packs to OEMs and integrators, and we sell our BMS to individual builders through the BetterBMS store as well as to OEMs under the EVDrive brand.
How is your BMS better than AEM, Vero, or Orion?
We built ours after years of being unsatisfied with each of those. The full comparison — features, safety, and configurability — lives on the BetterBMS site.
Why are your batteries priced above commodity packs?
Because they're engineered for class-leading power and energy density on premium 21700 chemistry, for buyers who need the performance. If commodity capacity is the goal, we're honest that we're not the cheapest option.
Do you still do EV conversions?
Only select high-end builds. Our focus is battery systems and BMS, not standard low-cost conversions.
