Flagship · New

Choose BetterBMS
Over AEM, Vero
or Orion

A battery management system is only as good as the pack it protects. Ours was developed inside EVDrive packs for five years before it shipped, so the integration, the safety logic, and the support all come from the people who built the cells it watches.

01

True cell-level monitoring

Per-cell voltage and temperature sensing across the full pack, not block-level averages. You see the one cell that drifts before it becomes the cell that fails, which is exactly where AEM and entry Orion tiers stop short.

02

Fault tolerance and safety

Redundant sensing paths, isolation monitoring, contactor control, and graded fault response that derates before it disconnects. Safety logic proven in record-setting packs on track, not modeled on a bench.

03

Configurability that fits real builds

Cell chemistry, pack topology, current limits, thermal thresholds, and CAN mapping are all configurable. Vero and Orion ask you to design around their fixed assumptions. BetterBMS adapts to the pack you are actually building.

04

Tight integration with EVDrive packs

When the same engineers build the cells, the cooling, and the management, nothing is reverse-engineered. Drop BetterBMS into an EVDrive pack and the calibration, balancing, and thermal model are already matched to the hardware.

05

Firmware and support from the source

Documented firmware, real update paths, and engineers who answer the phone because they wrote the code and built the pack. No forum-archaeology, no orphaned units. That continuity is the difference between a component vendor and a battery partner.
