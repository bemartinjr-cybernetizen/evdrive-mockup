BetterBMS · Flagship
The brain of the pack

Meet BetterBMS.

A battery management system developed across five years inside our own packs, then released to the builders and small OEMs who need management they can actually trust. Here is the honest case for choosing it over AEM, Vero, or Orion.

Cell-level monitoring

Per-cell voltage and temperature sensing across the full stack, not block averages. You see the one cell that is drifting before it becomes the cell that fails, which is exactly the resolution serious programs demand.

Fault tolerance & safety

Layered protection with redundant fault detection, defined safe states, and contactor control engineered for high-energy packs. Safety logic proven over five years in our own race and record builds, not adapted from a hobby board.

Configurability

Tunable cell chemistry profiles, thresholds, balancing strategy, and CAN mapping, so the same BMS adapts to a 45 kWh record pack or a compact builder pack without forcing a one-size compromise.

Native pack integration

Engineered alongside EVDrive packs, so sensing, balancing, and thermal logic are matched to the hardware from day one. With AEM, Vero, or Orion you integrate a third party. With BetterBMS the pack and the brain were designed together.

Firmware and support that stays with you. Maintained firmware, documented CAN, and direct access to the engineers who built it. When a question comes up at midnight before a race, you reach the people who designed the system, not a queue.
